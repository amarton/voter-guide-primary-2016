KEY DATES

Feb. 12: Deadline for a registered voter to file in circuit court a petition challenging the residency of a candidate.


April 5: Deadline to register to vote or change party affiliation for the primary election


April 14-April 21: Early voting for primary election


April 26: Primary election


July 18-July 21: Republican National Convention


July 25-July 29: Democratic National Convention


Oct. 18: Deadline to register to vote in general election


Oct. 27-Nov. 3: Early voting for general election


Nov. 8: General election
