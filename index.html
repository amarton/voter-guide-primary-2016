    MARYLAND VOTER GUIDE 2016     
Marylanders will elect a new U.S. Senator and several U.S. congressional members in 2016. Citizens in Baltimore will vote for a new mayor, comptroller and for city council members. The primary election was held April 26. This voter guide will be updated with general election information after the results of the primary are certified.
